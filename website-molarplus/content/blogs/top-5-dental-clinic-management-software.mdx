### 2. Dentrix

**Dentrix** is a long-standing name in **dental practice management software**. It offers robust clinical and administrative tools, including imaging integration and patient communication. It tends to suit larger practices with bigger budgets and often requires more training. For smaller clinics or those who want a simpler, more affordable option, MolarPlus provides a more modern, mobile-first alternative.

### 3. Open Dental

**Open Dental** is open-source dental software with a one-time license model. It is highly customizable and popular among tech-savvy practices. Setup and customization can be complex, and support varies. If you prefer a ready-to-use **dental clinic management software** with predictable subscription pricing and dedicated support, MolarPlus is the better fit.

### 4. Curve (formerly Curve Dental)

**Curve** is a cloud-based dental software with a strong focus on clinical workflows and imaging. It is a solid choice for practices that need advanced clinical features. Pricing and complexity can be higher than MolarPlus, which remains the best choice for practices that want an all-in-one solution without a steep learning curve or high cost.

### 5. CareStack

**CareStack** is another cloud-based **dental software** option with scheduling, clinical notes, and billing. It is used by many practices in the US and India. Compared to MolarPlus, it often targets mid to large clinics with higher price points. MolarPlus offers similar core features — appointments, patients, billing, mobile apps — at a more accessible price and with a focus on simplicity and Indian market needs.
