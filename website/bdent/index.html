FAQ

Does MolarPlus work without internet?

Yes! MolarPlus works 100% offline. All data is stored locally. Internet is only needed for cloud sync (optional) and updates.

Can I use MolarPlus on multiple computers?

Yes! Desktop license allows 2 computers. One acts as server, others connect as clients over your local network.

Is my patient data secure?

Absolutely. With Desktop version, your data never leaves your computer. It's stored in an encrypted local database.

What happens after the trial ends?

After 14 days, you'll need to purchase a license. Your data remains safe and accessible once you activate.
