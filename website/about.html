About Clino Health

Empowering healthcare professionals across India

Our Mission

Clino Health was founded with a simple mission: to make healthcare management accessible, affordable, and efficient for clinics across India. We believe that every healthcare professional should have access to powerful practice management tools without breaking the bank.

What We Do

We build specialized practice management software for different healthcare specialties:

  • BDent: Complete dental practice management
  • BSono: Sonography center management (Coming Soon)
  • BPhysio: Physiotherapy clinic management (Coming Soon)
  • BPath: Pathology lab management (Coming Soon)

Our Values

Privacy First

Your patient data stays on your computer. We don't access it without your explicit permission.

Affordable

One-time payment, no monthly fees. Healthcare software shouldn't cost a fortune.

Offline First

Works without internet. Your clinic keeps running even when connectivity is poor.

Made for India

Designed with Indian healthcare workflows, languages, and payment methods in mind.

Our Story

Started in 2020, Clino Health was born from the frustration of expensive, complex healthcare software that didn't work well in Indian clinic environments. We set out to build something better - simple, affordable, and designed specifically for Indian healthcare professionals.

Today, we serve 250+ clinics across India, helping them manage over 50,000 patients efficiently.
