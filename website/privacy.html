Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Clino Health ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our software products.

2. Data Collection

Clino Health software stores all patient data locally on your computer. We do not collect, transmit, or store your patient data on our servers unless you explicitly opt-in to cloud backup services.

We may collect the following information:

  • License information for software activation
  • Anonymous usage statistics (with your consent)
  • Support requests and communication

3. Data Storage

All patient data, medical records, and clinic information are stored locally on your computer. Your data never leaves your system unless you explicitly choose to use cloud backup features.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, you are responsible for:

  • Maintaining secure backups of your data
  • Using strong passwords for your system
  • Keeping your software updated
  • Following HIPAA and local data protection regulations

5. Third-Party Services

Clino Health may use third-party services for:

  • Payment processing (Razorpay, Stripe)
  • Email notifications (if enabled)
  • Software updates

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your data from the software
  • Opt-out of anonymous usage statistics

7. Children's Privacy

Our software is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
